# LifeSim: Life After College
> **Level up your career. Balance your budget.**

## About The Game
LifeSim is a text-based web game that puts you in the shoes of a recent college graduate. Your goal is to survive 6 years of real-world financial choices, stressful business trips, and unpredictable life curveballs without letting your stress completely max out!

## Cool Features
* **Track Your Stats:** Balance your Bank Account, Student Debt, Career Level, and Stress.
* **The Travel App:** Use the virtual "Lockton Travel Companion" to save yourself from delayed flights and spilled coffee.
* **Color-Changing UI:** Watch your stress bar change from green to red, and pulse a warning when you are too overwhelmed.
* **Realistic Spending:** If you spend money you don't have, it acts like a credit card and automatically adds to your debt!

## Built With
I built this from scratch using the basics of web development:
* **HTML:** To build the structure of the game.
* **CSS:** To design the cards, buttons, and animations.
* **JavaScript:** To make the game work, calculate the math, and update the screen when you click a button.
